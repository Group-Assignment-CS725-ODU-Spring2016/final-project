Project Overview

Hearing Conservation Program (HCP) which is run by US navy aims to track hearing level of workers over a period of time.

HCP collected audiogram data for about 150k workers for a period of 20 years.

We are trying to design visualizations that allow users to analyze hearing curves among the workers.
